Earth’s Finest Morning

With Tim Robards and Anna Heinrich

FIJI Water is bringing a slice of tropical paradise to Sydney for one weekend only with Earth’s Finest Morning – an exclusive, free Saturday morning retreat for the body and soul.

Join Sydney’s favourite couple, Tim Robards and Anna Heinrich, for an intimate morning of fitness and nutrition at the FIJI Water Weekender, popping up at one of Sydney’s most stunning private residences in Darling Point.

Throw on your active wear and we will take care of the rest – this is your time to put the worries of the week behind you and kick off your weekend on the right note.

For the first time ever, Tim and Anna will work together to present a fitness collaboration that will put guests through their paces in an exclusive workout session before hosting a delicious and nutritious brunch – all set to a stunning backdrop of harbour views.

If the weather is kind, we recommend packing your bathers and of course we’ll have plenty of FIJI Water on hand to keep you hydrated!

About FIJI Water

FIJI™ Water, natural artesian water bottled at the source in Viti Levu (Fiji islands), is the No. 1 imported bottled water in the United States. FIJI Water, known for its iconic square bottle, soft mouth feel and unique mineral profile, is the water of choice among discerning consumers and top chefs.  Widely available at fine restaurants, hotels, and all major retail channels, FIJI Water has expanded globally to more than 60 countries.
