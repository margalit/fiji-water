About FIJI Water

FIJI™ Water, natural artesian water bottled at the source in Viti Levu (Fiji islands), is the No. 1 imported bottled water in the United States. FIJI Water, known for its iconic square bottle, soft mouth feel and unique mineral profile, is the water of choice among discerning consumers and top chefs.  Widely available at fine restaurants, hotels, and all major retail channels, FIJI Water has expanded globally to more than 60 countries. To discover Earth’s Finest Water™, please visit www.fijiwater.com.au and like us on Facebook.

What is Earth’s Finest Morning?

Earth’s Finest Morning is an exclusive, free Saturday morning retreat for the body and soul. Hosted by well-known celebrity couple Tim Robards and Anna Heinrich, the morning will comprise of an exercise session followed by a delicious and nutritious brunch. It will be held on Saturday 25th March at a secret location at Darling Point, Sydney.

How can I attend?

Earth’s Finest Morning is a free ticketed event but like all good things, places are strictly limited. Register your interest now to go into the ballot to receive tickets for you and a friend.

When is Earth’s Finest Morning?

Saturday 25th March, 9am to 12noon.

Where is the FIJI Water Weekender?

The event will take place at a private residence in Darling Point, Sydney. The exact location is a secret and will be revealed to attendees prior to the event. You will not be disappointed!

How do I get to the FIJI Water Weekender?

Transport to and from the event is the sole responsibility of the attendees. FIJI Water will not be providing any form of transport to or from the event. All successful entrants will receive details of the secret event location once their attendance is confirmed so they can plan their journey.

Street parking is available.

Is there a cost for a ticket?

No, Earth’s Finest Morning is free of charge to attend however places are strictly limited. To attend, register your interest for you and a friend to attend by visiting www.earthsfinestmorning.com Successful applicants will be selected by ballot.

When does the ballot close?

The ballot closes at 23:59 Thursday 16 March 2017.

When will winners be notified?

10 successful entries will be drawn at random on 17 March 2017 and notified by FIJI Water via an email sent to the registered email address.

Can I bring a friend?

Yes! All 10 successful entries will win a place for themselves and a guest at Earth’s Finest Morning.

Do I need to be 18 years of age to enter?

Yes, the ballot is only open to people aged 18 years of age and over.

What should I wear?

As you will be taking part in an exercise session with Tim Robards and Anna Heinrich please wear appropriate active wear including runners. You are more than welcome to bring a change of clothes to change into after the exercise session.

What should I bring?

Please bring along active wear including runners for the exercise session. Don’t forget your camera to take lots of photos and swim suit if you fancy a post workout dip.

There’s no need to bring food or drink along. A deliciously healthy post work out brunch will be supplied and there will of course be plenty of FIJI Water on hand.

What if I have dietary requirements?

No problem! All successful entrants will be asked for any dietary requirements ahead of the day.

Do I have to take part in the exercise session?

No. It is a completely optional activity however as it is a main element of Earth’s Finest Morning we encourage all guests to give it a go.

Can I take photos at the event?

Of course! We will be encouraging guests to take lots of photos and share them on social media #FIJIWater #EarthsFinestMorning
